  1. We must not lift the pen from the paper during the drawing process, which implies that z(γt)z(\gamma_t)z(γt​) must be continuous.

  2. The velocity of the pen point must be well-defined and finite, and it must also vary continuously. Therefore, z′(t)z'(t)z′(t) must exist and be continuous on the interval [a,b][a, b][a,b].

Definition (ppp-norm). Given p∈N+p \in \mathbb{N}^+p∈N+ and a random variable X∈LpX \in L_pX∈Lp​, define

∥X∥p≐(E[∣X∣p])1/p. \Vert X \Vert_p \doteq \Big( \mathbb{E} \big[ |X|^p \big] \Big)^{1 / p}. ∥X∥p​≐(E[∣X∣p])1/p.

We verify that this is a norm.
Denote a=∥X∥pa = \Vert X \Vert_pa=∥X∥p​, b=∥Y∥pb = \Vert Y \Vert_pb=∥Y∥p​ and s=a+bs = a + bs=a+b.
Via the convexity of ∣⋅∣p| \cdot |^p∣⋅∣p,

∥as⋅Xa+bs⋅Yb∥pp=E[∣as⋅Xa+bs⋅Yb∣p]≤E[as⋅∣Xa∣p+bs⋅∣Yb∣p]=as+bs=1. \begin{aligned} \left\Vert \frac{a}{s} \cdot \frac{X}{a} + \frac{b}{s} \cdot \frac{Y}{b} \right\Vert_p^p &= \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \left\vert \frac{a}{s} \cdot \frac{X}{a} + \frac{b}{s} \cdot \frac{Y}{b} \right\vert^p \Bigg] \\ &\le \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \frac{a}{s} \cdot \left\vert \frac{X}{a} \right\vert^p + \frac{b}{s} \cdot \left\vert \frac{Y}{b} \right\vert^p \Bigg] \\ &= \frac{a}{s} + \frac{b}{s} \\ &= 1. \end{aligned} ∥∥∥∥∥​sa​⋅aX​+sb​⋅bY​∥∥∥∥∥​pp​​=E[∣∣∣∣∣​sa​⋅aX​+sb​⋅bY​∣∣∣∣∣​p]≤E[sa​⋅∣∣∣∣∣​aX​∣∣∣∣∣​p+sb​⋅∣∣∣∣∣​bY​∣∣∣∣∣​p]=sa​+sb​=1.​

Further, for q,p∈N+,q≤pq, p \in \mathbb{N}^+, q \le pq,p∈N+,q≤p, we have ∥X∥q≤∥X∥p\Vert X \Vert_q \le \Vert X \Vert_p∥X∥q​≤∥X∥p​, since via the convexity of ∣⋅∣p/q| \cdot |^{p / q}∣⋅∣p/q,

∥X∥qp=(E[∣X∣q])p/q≤E[(∣X∣q)p/q]=∥X∥pp. \Vert X \Vert_q^p = \Big( \mathbb{E} \big[ |X|^q \big] \Big)^{p / q} \le \mathbb{E} \Big[ \big( |X|^q \big)^{p / q} \Big] = \Vert X \Vert_p^p. ∥X∥qp​=(E[∣X∣q])p/q≤E[(∣X∣q)p/q]=∥X∥pp​.

Khintchine Inequality. Given p∈N+p \in \mathbb{N}^+p∈N+, a⃗=(a1,…,an)∈Rn\vec{a} = (a_1, \ldots, a_n) \in \mathbb{R}^na=(a1​,…,an​)∈Rn and independent Rademacher random variable X1,…,XnX_1, \ldots, X_nX1​,…,Xn​,

∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥p≤p+1p⋅p⋅∥a⃗∥2∈O(p⋅∥a⃗∥2). \left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_p \le \frac{\sqrt{p + 1}}{\sqrt{p}} \cdot \sqrt{p} \cdot \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2 \in O( \sqrt{p} \cdot \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2 ). ∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​i∈[n]∑​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​p​≤p​p+1​​⋅p​⋅∥a∥2​∈O(p​⋅∥a∥2​).

Proof. Suppose that this holds when ppp is even.
Then for odd ppp, by monotonicity of random variable norms,

∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥p≤∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥p+1∈O(p+1⋅∥a⃗∥2)=O(p⋅∥a⃗∥2). \left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_p \le \left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_{p + 1} \in O \left( \sqrt{p + 1} \cdot \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2 \right) = O \left( \sqrt{p} \cdot \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2 \right). ∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​i∈[n]∑​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​p​≤∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​i∈[n]∑​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​p+1​∈O(p+1​⋅∥a∥2​)=O(p​⋅∥a∥2​).

It is left to consider that ppp is even.
Let Z1,…,ZnZ_1, \ldots, Z_nZ1​,…,Zn​ be independent standard normal random variable.
We compare ∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥p\left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_p∥∥∥∥​∑i∈[n]​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥​p​ with ∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Zi∥p\left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i \right\Vert_p∥∥∥∥​∑i∈[n]​ai​⋅Zi​∥∥∥∥​p​.
Observe that both

\begin{align*} \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \Bigg| \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \Bigg|^p \Bigg] = \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \Bigg( \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \Bigg)^p \Bigg], \\ \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \Bigg| \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i \Bigg|^p \Bigg] = \mathbb{E} \Bigg[ \Bigg( \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i \Bigg)^p \Bigg], \end{align*}

can be expanded into a sum of expectation of monomials.

Each monomial with odd exponents has expectation 000.
Meanwhile, for non-negative even integers m1,…,mnm_1, \ldots, m_nm1​,…,mn​ that sum up to ppp, we have

∏i∈[n](ai)miE[(Xi)mi]=∏i∈[n](ai)mi≤∏i∈[n](ai)miE[(Zi)mi], \prod_{i \in [n]} (a_i)^{m_i} \mathbb{E}[ \left( X_i \right)^{m_i} ] = \prod_{i \in [n]} (a_i)^{m_i} \le \prod_{i \in [n]} (a_i)^{m_i} \mathbb{E}[ \left( Z_i \right)^{m_i} ], i∈[n]∏​(ai​)mi​E[(Xi​)mi​]=i∈[n]∏​(ai​)mi​≤i∈[n]∏​(ai​)mi​E[(Zi​)mi​],

Therefore ∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥pp\left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_p^p∥∥∥∥​∑i∈[n]​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥​pp​ is dominated term-by-term by ∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Zi∥pp\left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i \right\Vert_p^p∥∥∥∥​∑i∈[n]​ai​⋅Zi​∥∥∥∥​pp​.
But ∑i∈[n]ai⋅Zi\sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i∑i∈[n]​ai​⋅Zi​ follows the distribution N(0,∥a⃗∥2)N(0, \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2)N(0,∥a∥2​).
Therefore,

∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Xi∥p≤∥∑i∈[n]ai⋅Zi∥p=∥a⃗∥2⋅((p−1)!!)1/p \left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot X_i \right\Vert_p \le \left\Vert \sum_{i \in [n]} a_i \cdot Z_i \right\Vert_p = \Vert \vec{a} \Vert_2 \cdot \big( \left( p - 1\right)!! \big)^{1 / p} ∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​i∈[n]∑​ai​⋅Xi​∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​p​≤∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​i∈[n]∑​ai​⋅Zi​∥∥∥∥∥∥∥​p​=∥a∥2​⋅((p−1)!!)1/p

(p−1)!!≤pp/2⟹((p−1)!!)1/p≤p(p - 1) !! \le p^{p / 2} \Longrightarrow \big( \left( p - 1\right)!! \big)^{1 / p} \le \sqrt{p}(p−1)!!≤pp/2⟹((p−1)!!)1/p≤p​.

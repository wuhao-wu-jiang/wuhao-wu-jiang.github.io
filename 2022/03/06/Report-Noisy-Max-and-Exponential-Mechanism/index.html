Report Noisy Max and Exponential Mechanism

Posted on 2022-03-06 Edited on 2022-10-17

Let d∈N+d \in \mathbb{N}^+d∈N+ be a positive integer, and u⃗∈Nd\vec{u} \in \mathbb{N}^du∈Nd be a vector.

Laplacian Mechanism

Since ∥u⃗−v⃗∥∞=1\Vert \vec{u} - \vec{v} \Vert_\infty = 1∥u−v∥∞​=1, it is possible that ∥u⃗−v⃗∥1=d\Vert \vec{u} - \vec{v} \Vert_1 = d∥u−v∥1​=d.

Proof.

Parallel Compare

This operation compares a list of values $x_1, \ldots, x_k$ and $y_1, \ldots, y_k$ in parallel:

  1. Pack $x_1, \ldots, x_k$ into a
    machine word $X$, separated by $1$s.
  2. Pack $y_1, \ldots, y_k$ into a
    machine word $Y$, separated by $0$s.
  3. Compute $X – Y$. The bit preceding
    $x_i – y_i$ is $1$ if $x_i \ge y_i$ and $0$ otherwise.

Threshold Sampling

One possible implementation of this sampling scheme is called threshold sampling.

Background

Given a matrix A∈Rm×nA \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}A∈Rm×n, the goal of low approximation is to find a matrix B∈Rk×nB \in \mathbb{R}^{k \times n}B∈Rk×n with rank at most kkk that approximates AAA as well as possible. This can be measured by 222-norm, i.e.,

∣∣ATA−BTB∣∣=xTATAx−xTBTBx=∣∣Ax∣∣2−∣∣Bx∣∣2=∑i=1n(AiTx)2+∣∣Bi−1x∣∣2−∣∣Bix∣∣2=∑i=1n∣∣Σi−ViTx∣∣2−∣∣ΣiViTx∣∣2≤∑i=1n∣∣ViΣi−TΣi−ViT−ViΣiTΣiViT∣∣=∑i=1n∣∣Σi−TΣi−−ΣiTΣi∣∣=∑i=1nσi,k/22\begin{aligned} ||A^T A - B^T B|| &= x^TA^T A x- x^T B^T Bx \\ &= ||Ax||^2 - ||Bx||^2 \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n (A_i^T x)^2 + ||B_{i - 1} x||^2 - ||B_{i} x||^2 \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||\Sigma_{i-} V_i^T x||^2 - ||\Sigma_i V_i^T x||^2 \\ &\le \sum_{i = 1}^n ||V_i \Sigma_{i-}^T \Sigma_{i-} V_i^T - V_i \Sigma_i^T \Sigma_i V_i^T || \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||\Sigma_{i-}^T \Sigma_{i-} - \Sigma_i^T \Sigma_i || \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n \sigma_{i, k /2 }^2 \end{aligned} ∣∣ATA−BTB∣∣​=xTATAx−xTBTBx=∣∣Ax∣∣2−∣∣Bx∣∣2=i=1∑n​(AiT​x)2+∣∣Bi−1​x∣∣2−∣∣Bi​x∣∣2=i=1∑n​∣∣Σi−​ViT​x∣∣2−∣∣Σi​ViT​x∣∣2≤i=1∑n​∣∣Vi​Σi−T​Σi−​ViT​−Vi​ΣiT​Σi​ViT​∣∣=i=1∑n​∣∣Σi−T​Σi−​−ΣiT​Σi​∣∣=i=1∑n​σi,k/22​​

It is left to bound the size of ∑i=1nσi,k/22\sum_{i = 1}^n \sigma_{i, k /2 }^2∑i=1n​σi,k/22​:

∣∣A∣∣F−∣∣B∣∣F=∑i=1n∣∣Ai∣∣2+∣∣Bi−1∣∣F−∣∣Bi∣∣F=∑i=1n∣∣UiΣi−ViT∣∣F−∣∣ΣiViT∣∣F=∑i=1n∣∣Σi−ViT∣∣F−∣∣ΣiViT∣∣F=∑i=1n∣∣Σi−∣∣F−∣∣Σi∣∣F≥(k/2)∑i=1nσi,k/22\begin{aligned} ||A||_F - ||B||_F &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||A_i||^2 + ||B_{i - 1}||_F - ||B_{i}||_F \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||U_i \Sigma_{i-} V_i^T||_F - ||\Sigma_i V_i^T||_F \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||\Sigma_{i-} V_i^T||_F - ||\Sigma_i V_i^T||_F \\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^n ||\Sigma_{i-}||_F - ||\Sigma_i||_F \\ &\ge (k / 2) \sum_{i = 1}^n \sigma_{i, k /2 }^2 \end{aligned} ∣∣A∣∣F​−∣∣B∣∣F​​=i=1∑n​∣∣Ai​∣∣2+∣∣Bi−1​∣∣F​−∣∣Bi​∣∣F​=i=1∑n​∣∣Ui​Σi−​ViT​∣∣F​−∣∣Σi​ViT​∣∣F​=i=1∑n​∣∣Σi−​ViT​∣∣F​−∣∣Σi​ViT​∣∣F​=i=1∑n​∣∣Σi−​∣∣F​−∣∣Σi​∣∣F​≥(k/2)i=1∑n​σi,k/22​​

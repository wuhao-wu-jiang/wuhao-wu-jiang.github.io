Total Variance Distance

Posted on 2022-01-31 Edited on 2022-02-01

Let (Ω,F)(\Omega, \mathcal{F})(Ω,F) be a measurable space, μ\muμ and ν\nuν be two probability measure.

Definition. The total variance distance between μ\muμ and ν\nuν is defined as

∥μ−ν∥TV≐sup⁡E∈F∣μ(E)−ν(E)∣. \Vert \mu - \nu \Vert_{TV} \doteq \sup_{ E \in \mathcal{F} } \big| \mu(E) - \nu(E) \big|. ∥μ−ν∥TV​≐E∈Fsup​∣∣∣​μ(E)−ν(E)∣∣∣​.

  3. Let κ\kappaκ be another probability measure.
    Then
